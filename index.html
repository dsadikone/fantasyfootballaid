Welcome to Fantasy Football Aid

th This is an application that reads football statistics from a database and then gives recommendations for the best fantasy football team. It has an easy to use User Interface that makes it easy to see what the best players are at the moment. The first thing that you will see when you launch the application is shown below. The arrows allows you to navigate to the other screens in the program.


Here you can see all of the running backs that are in the database. You can switch between the Wide Receivers, Quarterback, and Running Back stats by clicking on the buttons At the bottom of the screen. You can also select any player to open a popup will all of that players stats. You see all of the same stats that are displayed on the table but since it is in a popup window, it is easier to compare with other players. There is also a button on the popup window that allows you to add the player to your team.


This is what your team look like in the application. When you add a player to your team he will show up on the "My Team" page. Here you can see all the players that you currently have on your team, along with the number of games they have played, the total number of points each player has, and the average amount each player has made per game. This is also where you can remove players from your team by selecting them and clicking on the Remove button.


Here you can see the Running Back page with multiple popups open. With the popups able to be moved throughout the screen it is easy to compare players with each other. The popups will all stay open until you click on the X in the top right corner of the popup. Using this popup system, it is easy to look thru a lot of players to find the best team possible.
